DBSC Aquarium Temporarily Closed:

Temporarily Closed: In light of the evolving situation unfolding around COVID-19 and following advice from government health officials advising Canadians to practice social distancing to flatten the curve, we have made the decision to temporarily close DBSC’s Aquarium of Canada. As the community copes with the fast-moving spread of COVID-19, effective Monday, March 16th at 6pm, DNSC Aquarium of Canada will temporarily close to the public to ensure the safety and well-being of our team members, guests and animals.
